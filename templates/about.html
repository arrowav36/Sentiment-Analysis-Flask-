About this Project

Naive Bayesian and decision list classifiers were used to classify a given review as positive or negative. Deep-learning neural networks is also popular in the area of sentiment analysis. My dataset comes from Consumer Reviews of Amazon Products

In this project i have used the naive bayes multinomial classifier, for more info. you can download the project from my github link .
